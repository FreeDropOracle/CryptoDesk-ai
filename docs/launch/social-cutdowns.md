# Social Video Cutdowns

Use these shorter edits to support the full beta demo.

## 60-Second Teaser

Goal:

- drive awareness
- send viewers to the beta signup page

Structure:

1. Hook: desktop crypto trading with clearer guardrails
2. Show dashboard and AI signal card
3. Show simulation mode briefly
4. End with beta CTA

Suggested CTA:

- Apply at `cryptodesk.ai/beta`
- Watch the full walkthrough at `cryptodesk.ai/demo`

## 30-Second Short

Goal:

- show the product quickly
- frame the product as safer and clearer than noisy exchange dashboards

Structure:

1. Problem: clutter and confusion
2. Product: cleaner UI plus explainable AI
3. Safety: simulation-first
4. CTA

## 15-Second Hook

Goal:

- stop the scroll
- create interest for the beta

Structure:

1. One-line problem statement
2. One-line product promise
3. CTA to the beta page

## Channel Notes

- X / Twitter: use the 60-second teaser
- YouTube Shorts / Reels: use the 30-second edit
- Paid or community promos: use the 15-second cut only if the message stays conservative

## Messaging Guardrails

- avoid profit claims
- avoid “AI trades for you” framing
- keep the words simulation-first, advisory-only, and non-custodial visible where appropriate
