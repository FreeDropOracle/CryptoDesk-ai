# Video Demo Plan

## Purpose

Create a clean, trustworthy demo video that helps beta applicants understand the product before applying.

## Primary Objectives

- explain the product clearly
- reduce trust friction
- show the simulation-first model
- reinforce the security posture
- drive qualified beta signups

## Deliverables

- full demo video: 5 to 7 minutes
- 60-second teaser
- 30-second short
- 15-second hook
- subtitles for English and Arabic

## Distribution Plan

| Channel | Format | Length | Primary Goal |
|--------|--------|--------|--------------|
| YouTube | Full demo | 5 to 7 min | Education |
| X / Twitter | Teaser | 60 sec | Awareness + CTA |
| Discord | Full demo link | 5 to 7 min | Community trust |
| Website / beta page | Embedded full demo | 5 to 7 min | Conversion |

## Success Signals

- viewers understand that the current beta is simulation-first
- video sends qualified users to the beta intake flow
- security model is easier to understand after watching
- comments and beta feedback show lower confusion about AI and live trading boundaries

## Production Notes

- record with safe demo data only
- prepare one English primary cut and subtitle it in Arabic, or vice versa
- keep the pace calm and credible rather than overly promotional
