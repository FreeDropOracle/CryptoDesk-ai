## Status Legend

- Accepted: invited into the active beta cohort
- Waitlist: not in the current cohort, but eligible later
- Rejected: not a fit for this round

## Review Tips

- Add one sentence explaining every waitlist or rejection decision
- Track language preference if bilingual feedback is important for the round
- Keep the sheet current enough that support and operations can act from it
