# Beta Testing Plan

## Goal
Validate the launch-readiness of CryptoDesk AI with a controlled cohort before any public release.

## Cohort
- Target size: 20 to 50 users
- Profile: simulation-first traders, security-conscious crypto users, and technical early adopters
- Geography: start with English- and Arabic-speaking testers who can provide structured feedback

## Duration
- Week 1: onboarding, setup, and first-session feedback
- Week 2: simulation trading and AI advisory feedback
- Week 3: stability, performance, and alert-stream validation
- Week 4: polish fixes and launch go/no-go review

## Recruitment Sources
- Private Discord trading communities
- Trusted crypto educator networks
- Existing waitlist or founder network
- Security-aware desktop app testers

## Success Metrics
- Fewer than 5 critical bugs across the full beta window
- Greater than 80% onboarding completion
- Greater than 60% week-over-week retained testers
- Clear qualitative trust in simulation labeling, AI explanations, and security posture

## Feedback Channels
- Dedicated Discord or Slack beta room
- Structured weekly survey
- In-app feedback capture in a later polish pass
- Short 1:1 interviews with the most active testers

## What To Observe
- Confusion around simulation vs live workflows
- Friction during credential setup and settings review
- Alert fatigue or unclear AI confidence messaging
- Performance complaints during market and AI updates
- Misunderstanding of risk filters or hold downgrades

## Exit Criteria
- Critical security blockers resolved
- Onboarding copy validated by testers
- Signed build distribution flow rehearsed successfully
- Known issues documented with mitigation or ship/no-ship decision
