# Email Launch Sequence

Use this sequence when the public or broader beta launch is actually ready.

## Email 1: Launch Or Beta Opening

Subject idea:

`CryptoDesk AI is now available`

Structure:

- short greeting
- one-sentence problem statement
- three product strengths
- demo link
- download or beta CTA
- short disclaimer footer

Suggested three strengths:

- explainable AI
- simulation-first safety
- non-custodial user control

## Email 2: Product Deep Dive

Subject idea:

`How CryptoDesk AI helps traders stay safer and clearer`

Focus on:

- simulation-first workflow
- explainable AI
- security model in plain language

CTA:

- watch the demo
- explore the docs
- join the community

## Email 3: Early Feedback And Trust

Subject idea:

`What we are learning from early users`

Focus on:

- early usage learnings
- what changed from feedback
- where the roadmap is heading next

CTA:

- share feedback
- join the next wave
- stay subscribed for release notes

## Footer Guidance

Include:

- website link
- demo link
- support or community link
- short risk disclaimer
