# Platform Variants

Use these platform-specific drafts as adaptations of the master launch announcement.

## X / Twitter Thread

Suggested flow:

1. Hook with product promise
2. Problem statement
3. Explainable AI angle
4. Simulation-first safety angle
5. Non-custodial angle
6. Demo link
7. Download or waitlist CTA
8. Disclaimer

Example opener:

> Crypto trading tools are often either too noisy, too opaque, or too risky. We built CryptoDesk AI to be clearer, safer, and more transparent.

Include:

- one demo clip or static screenshot
- `https://cryptodesk.ai/demo`
- `[release or waitlist placeholder]`

## LinkedIn Post

Tone:

- professional
- architecture-aware
- trust-focused

Angle:

- security-first design for serious retail users
- explainable AI instead of black-box automation
- non-custodial desktop workflow

Suggested CTA:

- Watch the demo
- Review the product docs
- Join the beta or download when available

## Reddit Post

Tone:

- honest
- builder-focused
- ready to answer technical questions

Suggested title:

`I built a security-first desktop crypto app with simulation trading and explainable AI`

Suggested structure:

- what problem the product solves
- why simulation-first matters
- why non-custodial matters
- what makes the AI explainable
- explicit disclaimer
- invite questions

## Discord Announcement

Keep it short:

- one-sentence launch note
- one visual or demo link
- one CTA
- one reminder that the current release track stays conservative and transparent

## Arabic Short Variant

Use this when posting to Arabic-speaking audiences:

> CryptoDesk AI هو تطبيق سطح مكتب يركز على الأمان، ويجمع بين المحاكاة أولاً، وتوصيات ذكاء اصطناعي قابلة للتفسير، وسيطرة كاملة للمستخدم على مفاتيحه محلياً. شاهد الفيديو ثم انضم إلى البيتا أو حمّل الإصدار عند توفره.

## Guardrails

- never imply guaranteed returns
- never call the current product an automatic trading bot
- never suggest that user funds or keys are remotely held
- always keep the simulation-first and advisory-only framing where relevant
