## Beta Intake Questions

- What operating system do you use most often?
- Do you prefer English or Arabic?
- What is your crypto experience level?
- Are you more interested in simulation trading, AI analysis, or product UX feedback?
- Can you commit to 2 to 4 weeks of structured beta feedback?

## Messaging Guardrails

- Do not market the current build as live auto-trading software
- Do not promise profits, accuracy guarantees, or low-risk returns
- Do not imply remote custody of user funds or credentials
- Do emphasize simulation-first safety, transparency, and feedback-driven improvement
